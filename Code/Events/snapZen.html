Event Details

SnapZen-Focus On The Extraordinary

09:30 - 12:30 PM
Purchase Ticket

Join us for a Photography Event! Explore Techniques, capture moments and connect with fellow enthusiasts. Dive into creativity and let your image speak. Don’t miss the chance to ignite your passion for photography!

Team size : 1 Member


    Rules and Regulations


  • Participants will have 45 mins to click their snaps around the BIT Campus on their chosen topic
  • Participants should bring their requirements on their own.
  • After Clicking the snaps Participants should assemble in allotted venue.
  • Google Form will be shared to the Participants after gathering in the venue to submit their clicked snaps.
  • If any Plagiarism is Found ,the participant will be directly eliminated from the event.
  • Snaps/Photos should be submitted in the google form in the given time.
  • The Photos Should be Original. No filters should be added
  • Submissions must be made in specified formats, such as PNG,JPEG,PDF,JPG.
  • The Photos should be neat and appealing.

    Evaluation Criteria


  • Composition
  • Lighting
  • Colour Consideration
  • Presentation
  • Exposure
  • Photographic Technique
  • Style
  • Framing

    Timing slots


  • 1 hour will be given ,45 mins for taking snaps and 15 minutes for gathering in the venue.

    Requirements


  • DSLR / Mobile Phone
